Tim Abbott (Feb 11 2019 at 19:33):

@niels yes, deactivating a realm logs out all clients.

Tim Abbott (Feb 11 2019 at 19:33):

I think if you're doing a nightly backup, you don't want to deactivate a realm for it; the reason we recommend realm deactivation for exports is primarily to avoid new messages being sent (etc.) while the backup runs.

Tim Abbott (Feb 11 2019 at 19:34):

But if you want an incredibly clean backup, you can just supervisorctl stop all to stop the server while you do the backup instead.
