Tim Abbott (Jul 12 2023 at 14:14):

@Akash Kumar Sharma we don't support using a different low-level webserver -- there's a lot of configuration we'd need to maintain to route traffic to the different Zulip services, but you're welcome to put an Apache reverse proxy in front of your Zulip server, see https://zulip.readthedocs.io/en/latest/production/deployment.html#putting-the-zulip-application-behind-a-reverse-proxy.
