Tim Abbott (Jul 06 2017 at 19:30):

If you're not doing that, you can invite users to join realms, but you can also point your users at a per-realm registration page for that realm like this: https://chat.zulip.org/register/tabbott/ (where tabbott here is the string_id for the realm)
