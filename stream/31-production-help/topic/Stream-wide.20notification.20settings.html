Alya Abbott (Aug 31 2021 at 22:10):

Which of the following options would be most useful? Or would you want different ones for different situations? CC @Ronny @Robert (ro-i)

  1. Stream-specific default notification settings, which can be manually overridden by users.
  2. Streams for which users cannot set personal notification settings at all, and have to use organization defaults.
  3. Something in between (1) and (2), e.g. streams for which users can add notifications but cannot remove them.

Tim Abbott (Sep 01 2021 at 00:21):

I see how this could be useful. One concern I have with a "subscribers always get emails for messages in this stream" feature is that there's a spam issue it could create. We don't want to put users in a situation where they don't have a clear path to unsubscribe from any category of notification emails.

One possible solution to that is that we'd change the "How to unsubscribe" text at the bottom of the email if such a configuration were enabled to be something like "This stream is configured to send email notifications to all subscribers. You can unsubscribe from the stream or deactivate your account to stop receiving these notifications.", with several of those being links.

@Ronny @Robert (ro-i) I'd be curious for your thoughts on that as a solution that balances the needs of users with those of the organization?
