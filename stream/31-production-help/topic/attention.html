Alex Vandiver (Jul 03 2023 at 20:30):

If you need to get their attention, you should @ them. By default, this will pop up a desktop notification and a push notification to their mobile device, as well as an email to them. But if they have decided that they don't want all of those, they can have turned any of those notification forms off -- because that's their preference. If you want to override their preferences, you will need to tell them to change them, or log in as them and set them yourself.
