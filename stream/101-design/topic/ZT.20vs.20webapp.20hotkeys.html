Topic: ZT vs webapp hotkeys

Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (Jul 17 2019 at 22:40):

When developing Zulip-term[inal] there's been quite some work to keep the keyboard shortcuts/hotkeys similar to the webapp. It seemed a good time to ask: do you like the ones ZT has added, or at least determine if they can be adapted/adjusted to get a good cross-app fit?

There are some cases where we simply use arrow keys to move around (cf A/D for stream movement?), but we also added keys akin to P/Esc for jumping to starred messages (f), and potentially another soon for mentions (very provisionally #). After some confusion on plans for keyboard shortcuts for s/S in the webapp, we now have z to toggle zooming in/out of a stream/topic or All-PMs/one-conversation; that's a little difficult to document/describe, but has felt pretty intuitive to use compared to s/S! The other recent addition has been t to toggle between stream-list and topic-list, but that feels like it will definitely need adjusting for a non-keyboard-only app, and pending ongoing changes in ZT.
