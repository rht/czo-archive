Yash RE (Feb 22 2020 at 06:00):

When you use a filter such as "sent by me", you some subset of messages. Now, within this subset, some messages might be from the same topic, but they may not be actually consecutive ie actually "foo" topic could have a message by A, then one by B, then one by C, then another by A.
Our current narrow logic is that if two messages are from the same topic, instead of showing the FRB twice, they're joined together. This makes sense because obviously, this is one thread, it looks better this way.
However, consider if A used sent by me filter in above example, then they see their two messages and it appears in that search as though they were actually sent consecutively, and this is somewhat weird/confusing.
