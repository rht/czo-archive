Topic: "Edited topic" mark

TheMatten (Jun 18 2020 at 05:49):

Currently, UI makes no difference between changing topic and editing message, always simply showing (EDITED) in it's header - this can be slightly confusing, because users (at least me) usually expect "edit" to mean "change in message body" - I can imagine that someone new to Zulip may have no idea what's going on when their message suddenly changes to (EDITED), even though they did not alter it all (but someone else e.g. moved it to better topic).

Would it make sense to have e.g. (EDITED TOPIC) mark for messages that only have their topic altered?
