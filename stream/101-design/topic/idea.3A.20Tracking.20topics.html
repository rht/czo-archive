Topic: idea: Tracking topics

Sean McIlvenna (Jun 06 2017 at 19:46):

I've been looking for a way to track/watch specific topics. Currently it is a little hard to do this. In a large chat, where there are many conversations, it is easy to lose track (in the history) of conversations you are part of.
I'd like to suggest two features:
1) A chat filter similar to "Starred messages", where it shows you all of the topics you have participated in.
2) A feature similar to "Starred messages", but for "Starred topics", where the entire topic is starred, rather than just one message within the topic. I don't see flagging a single message as very useful, but flagging an entire topic would be very useful.

Robert Hönig (Jun 06 2017 at 19:49):

I find especially the first proposal very useful. Regarding the second: You can somewhat star a topic by starring any message in it and then clicking on it's respective topic in Starred messages, though I see why this seems a little hacky/non-straightforward.
