David Martin (Apr 13 2018 at 19:24):

@Nikhil Kumar Mishra This is a fundamental point to me.

I have hired and work with a number of UX/UI design and I and my UX/UI associates believe that systems which don't have the ability to expand this area simply have bad UX/UI design.

Regarding standardisation, consider this: Every OS (Mac, Linux and Windows) have the ability within their file explorers to easily increase or decrease the size of fields. (Think of Finder, Windows Explorer, etc). Imagine if they didn't! Imagine if you had a file called: "Zulip documentation 13-04-18.odt" and you could only see: "Z....04-18" as the entire filename. That's what it can feel like with streams!
