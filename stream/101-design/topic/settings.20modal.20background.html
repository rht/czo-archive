Should we make our settings and stream settings background white? I started thinking about it in the context of the link color discussion on #12321.

Every other product I can think of has a white settings page (even gmail, which used to have a lightly colored one). One benefit of us matching that pattern are that button colors, link colors, etc don't have to be redesigned just for the settings page. Also I think white will look more modern.
