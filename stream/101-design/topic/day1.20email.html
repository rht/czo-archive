Rishi Gupta (Oct 19 2017 at 01:55):

Currently we send our day1 email an hour after the user signs up. While I think I understand the motivation (let the user play around with stuff before they see the welcome email), I feel like that has a much higher chance of feeling like spam than if the email is more clearly transactional (sent as a part of the signup process, i.e. with no delay).

Also, I think an hour is rarely the right time frame for re-engaging with a product. if I'm checking out a product, I'm usually giving it something like 10 minutes; by an hour later I'm on to something else.
