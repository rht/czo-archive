I think we really ought to look into implementing something like svg-sprite. As I understand it, the main reason to do SVG-to-font conversion is to save on what would be tons of resource requests. There doesn't seem to be any other benefit, and a lot of pain--from issues like this, to having to eyeball through sizing matters, to--as Vlad and I have discovered--apparent rounding errors in the conversion process that can wildly exaggerate minor, otherwise unnoticeable variations in stroke width.
