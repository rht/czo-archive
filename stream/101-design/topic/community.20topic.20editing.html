Topic: community topic editing

Tim Abbott (May 04 2018 at 18:35):

I just enabled a new feature on this realm, called "community topic editing". Now, anyone can edit topics of messages other users sent in the last day (I think). It's not necessarily the right model for an organization of this size, but the theory is that it's a good default for newer, small organizations. So we may turn it back off in this organization in particular. (Organization admins have always had the ability to edit topics of other users' messages).
