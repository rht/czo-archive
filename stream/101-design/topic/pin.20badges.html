Alicja Raszkowska (Apr 20 2017 at 20:46):

I usually decoupage them
- print on regular paper using a laser printer
- glue it to a plastic sheet with decoupage glue (transparent plastic file folders work great)
- paste wide transparent scotch tape on top of the print
- cut everything out
- use decoupage glue or varnish around the cutout
- attach a pin on the back (I have just the pin parts, without the round part)

as far as the cost goes:
- one plastic file folder is enough for about ~40-60 pins or half as many pairs of earrings
