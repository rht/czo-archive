Topic: retain message in compose box

Vishnu KS (Sep 14 2017 at 20:33):

If compose box gets closed with a message on it and I open it again I would like to see the message that I typed restored on compose box. Its kind of painful to open the drafts and restore the message. Thoughts?

Steve Howell (Sep 14 2017 at 20:37):

I think we debated this for a while a few months ago. I'm personally against auto-restoring drafts, because 90% of the time my draft is "n" from typing a hotkey while the compose box is open. I think people proposed some heuristic where if the message is too small, you don't restore it, but I feel like that leads to inconsistent behavior.
