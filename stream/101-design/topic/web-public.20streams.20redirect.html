Alya Abbott (May 24 2022 at 22:14):

In #21942, we implemented a change so that when a spectator logs in, their location within Zulip is preserved. However, we did not do this for users who sign up rather than logging in.

Tim Abbott 's comment was that redirecting in case of sign-up would mean that the user wouldn't land on the Welcome Bot thread. However, I really think that we should still preserve the spectator's location in this situation, so that they can actually complete whatever action they wanted to do that caused them to click "sign up".

Zulip is (we hope!) perfectly usable without reading Welcome Bot right away, and those messages will still be there (as unreads) for later reference.

Thoughts? CC @Aman Agrawal (amanagr)
