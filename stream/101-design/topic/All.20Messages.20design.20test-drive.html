Vlad Korobov (Feb 13 2022 at 00:20):

How do we reason about the color choices for PMs and mentions?

Calm violet - kind of branding color (we use it also for icons).
image.png

This is about a person. It/s colorful, but not that vibrant as blue, also blue is a focus color and we don't want to mix them. Text buttons are also of that color: image.png.

Violet+ and bold label is when you see your mention. Just a bit brighter that calm purple so you would be happy when you see your name:
image.png

Since you can be mentioned in all kinds of contexts this violet background is contrast enough even when its close to other very light colors (we don't want a user to miss the mention)

Aquamarine (bluish green) for group mentions
As I understood we can distinguish group mention from user mention. So it's nice to have a group highlighted differently, potentially not that strongly as user. So "calm Aquamarine" here is close to user mention but different.
image.png

Aquamarine+ and bold label to highlight group mentions in which you are in. The distinctiveness of the background color is already proven by current PMs
image.png

PMs should be different from mentions and I think the nature of PMs should be a bit warmer. So I tried a warmer color for them. It is still close to grey, but its just a warm grey :smile:
image.png
