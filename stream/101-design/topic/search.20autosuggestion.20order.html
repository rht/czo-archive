Tim Abbott (Aug 19 2022 at 22:25):

I think the challenge is different users have different workflows and thus different expectations for what to do with the search box -- if you want to use search as just a general "fast navigation to a conversation" widget, then you want pm-with to take priority. If you're doing a search for a particular message, "sender" is more often what you're looking for.

Tim Abbott (Aug 19 2022 at 22:26):

In an ideal world, we figure out how to make the UI fast for both use cases; resorting between "Sender" and "private messages with" basically swaps which of those workflows we're making nice.
