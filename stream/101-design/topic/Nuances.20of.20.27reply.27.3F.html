Topic: Nuances of 'reply'?

Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (Jan 19 2021 at 00:14):

Just a thought, but have we considered UI alternatives to "reply" for streams/topics? Or maybe also for PMs too?

When considering Zulip "threaded", then some people appear to take "replies" as having some context of the replied-to message (like HN or reddit, or nested email replies by ID?), whereas it is in reality just a reply to the user/group/stream/topic.

The compose area should already make it obvious enough who/where one is "replying to" (there is no other context), but I wondered if a different term here could make this clearer.

Other systems perhaps avoid this through implicit narrow context, with the compose box always open and only pointing to the that narrow, so where a message goes is "right here" ^

Tim Abbott (Jan 19 2021 at 22:46):

Interesting question. I do think it's valuable that we support posting to a different place than one's looking at (I use this e.g. to send PMs to the sender of a message on a thread for private feedback on something, as well as various other use cases). That is of course a rare corner case -- probably 1% of messages are sent that way.
