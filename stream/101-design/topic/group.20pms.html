Tim Abbott (May 08 2017 at 18:03):

I think conceptually, we probably want "name this group PM thread" to actually be "create a private stream with this group of people, and possibly this message history".

The main thing that is really tricky from a UI perspective is how to handle the moving-history side of it, since you can imagine that a set of 3 people might have a wide range of different things they talk about, and not really intend their argument about whether to let Fred join their trip go into the trip planning thread which they then add Fred to (or whatever).

Tim Abbott (May 08 2017 at 18:04):

So there needs to be some mechanism for controlling what subset of the potentially thousands of messages of history with a set of people go into the new stream
