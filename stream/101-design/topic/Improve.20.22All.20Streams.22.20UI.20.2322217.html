PR #22252 adds a "+" sign for the unsubscribed streams replacing the on-hover checkmark.

Screenshots:
light-theme.jpg dark-theme.jpg

Videos:
light-theme.webm dark-theme.webm

For the dark theme:

  • I used the colors from the "mute topic" button in recent topics for the "+" sign.
  • For the disabled color (for private unsubscribed streams), I just used the regular-state color with 0.5 opacity.

For the light theme:

  • I used the 2 colors of the on-hover checkmark that is currently used for unsubscribed streams (hsl(0, 0%, 87%) and hsl(0, 0%, 72%)).
  • For the disabled color, I used hsl(0, 0%, 27%).

I wanted to get feedback for the colors used here.
