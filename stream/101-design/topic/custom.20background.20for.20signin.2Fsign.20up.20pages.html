Tim Abbott (Aug 15 2017 at 22:45):

My main uncertainty is whether this is something folks would actually use; many organizations don't have a pretty image of that size associated with them.

Brock Whittaker (Aug 15 2017 at 22:47):

It's probably a cool feature in some cases but I think it's particularly low value given that as Tim said, I don't think most orgs have a bg-friendly image associated with them. I think it also serves to clutter interface visually in an unpredictable way – given that people can upload whatever image they want to.

We would then have to worry about things like enforcing min-size or quality so we aren't stretching 100px photos. I just don't think it's worth implementing.

Rishi Gupta (Aug 15 2017 at 22:55):

I think I am more on the pro side; I think orgs like the recurse center, etc would have the resources to do a good job, and it could feel pretty high value to have a strongly branded "landing" page.

That being said, I agree that it could be a fair amount of work to make it generically look good.

Steve Howell (Aug 15 2017 at 22:58):
