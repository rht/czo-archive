Stream: design

Topic: part of a topic moved

Alya Abbott (Jan 05 2022 at 07:49):

In #20575, a user reported that the "This topic was moved by..." notification is confusing when only some messages from a topic are moved. I agree that we could use phrasing that makes it more clear what's going on. Thoughts on what wording to use? Suggestions in the GitHub discussion so far (assuming the "by" part was left out in the discussion by accident):

  1. "Part of this topic was moved by ... to ..."
  2. "3 messages from this topic were moved by ... to ..."
  3. "A conversation was moved by ... to ..."

Aman (amanagr) (Jan 05 2022 at 08:09):

Since to is more important than by here, we can place it first.
Part of the conversation above was moved to ... by ...

Greg Price (Jan 05 2022 at 22:05):

I like the variation that has a count of the messages. That's helpful for gauging the difference between "a two-message start of a tangent" and "the last week of discussion with 100 messages". (The latter sounds extreme but has legitimate use cases, like when a complex discussion pivots into a different complex discussion and then you want to continue both of them.)

Tim Abbott (Jan 05 2022 at 22:52):

Yeah, I would appreciate having a count as well.

Alex Vandiver (Jan 05 2022 at 22:57):

I sometimes have to piecemeal pick apart a topic and move only parts of it. Since we don't have a "select some" U Ito move messagfes, that means that in practice I either:

  • Move everything below a point, and then move some things back
  • Move individual messages one-at-a-time

While I generally also want a count, either of those is going to be misleading with a count. Which is probably more of an argument for having a "move some" UI than one against having a count on the moved message.

Tim Abbott (Jan 05 2022 at 23:04):

Yeah, I think we should assume we're going to build a move-some feature; the main limitation in doing so is just figuring out a good way to implement the "Select the messages you want" experience.

Alya Abbott (Jan 05 2022 at 23:21):

Sounds good; I posted the following comment on the original issue:

Based on discussion on CZO, to solve this issue we should change the text to the following format when only part of the topic is moved:

3 messages from this topic were moved to ... by ... .

When all messages are moved, we should reorder the message to be:

This topic was moved to ... by ... .
