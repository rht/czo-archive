Stream: design

Topic: url friendly stream names

Rishi Gupta (Jul 31 2017 at 02:25):

I refer to specific streams and topics a lot outside of the zulip UI; e.g. suggesting to someone they should make an account and introduce themselves on #new members, or telling someone their issue is being discussed on some topic, or linking to a topic from github.

In these cases, the spaces in streams/topics make for awkward things like having to refer to streams as "#new members", or pasting URLs with a bunch of #20s.

I think we should consider ' ' and '-' equivalent for stream and topic names. Thoughts?

Joshua Pan (Jul 31 2017 at 02:28):

This is kind of similar with emoji names, where you can now call them with underscores or spaces

Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (Jul 31 2017 at 02:59):

I had a related issue with poll bot, where I wanted to be able to refer to a stream with a space. I settled on '+' for now, but if there was an official substitute that'd be good to settle on.

Steve Howell (Jul 31 2017 at 08:49):

I like dashes as the canonical replacement for spaces, and we could enforce that no two streams had the same canonical representation.

Tim Abbott (Jul 31 2017 at 14:36):

I guess the other option is _, but I think - is nicer both visually and in terms of typing.

Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (Jul 31 2017 at 15:34):

Doesn't this rule out those characters in stream names?

Steve Howell (Jul 31 2017 at 16:18):

No it only prevents duplicates where space and dash are different. Kind of like case insensitivity.
