Topic: tooltip for EDITED/MOVED

Tim Abbott (Apr 29 2022 at 18:57):

Currently, when you hover over the MOVED/EDITED notice in a message that has been moved or edited, we display via an HTML title tooltip (i.e. the slow, unstyled kind) the date that the message was last moved. E.g. "Moved (Apr 27 at 10:08PM)". And we hint that clicking it might do something via hover styling, but there's nothing else that indicates that. I think it could be nicer to do a 2-line Tippy tooltip on those elements:

Moved on Apr 27 at 10:10PM.
Click to view full edit history.

Tim Abbott (Apr 29 2022 at 18:59):

Migrating to a Tippy tooltip is helpful both for speed of access of the information and also for consistency with the most similar message controls (the timestamp and top-right hover menus).

Tim Abbott (Apr 29 2022 at 19:00):

There's probably a broader question of whether we want to migrate the other remaining UI controls in messages to Tippy; I think the main one that isn't is the tooltip if you hover over a user's avatar; that one is a much larger click target and thus has more risk of being annoying (E.g. because your most happened to rest on that element)

Alya Abbott (Apr 29 2022 at 19:51):

I'm not sure, but we probably want the tippy tooltips, with a delay to make sure they don't pop up unnecessarily.
