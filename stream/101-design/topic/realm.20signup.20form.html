  • Make everything vertical. E.g. Password and Organization Name look like they should be related in this layout

Steve Howell (Jun 16 2017 at 00:46):

Vertical makes sense to me. I kinda think the user's email and password should be more toward the bottom, especially the password. The password can be the most annoying thing to decide, so it makes sense to have it last, once the user already has invested time. I also think it's sort of logical to have the org stuff first.

Greg Price (Jun 16 2017 at 00:47):

Agreed on everything in the first two bullets. I think I prefer to the right (or the left) rather than tooltips.

Greg Price (Jun 16 2017 at 00:47):

Steve Howell (Jun 16 2017 at 00:50):

Greg Price (Jun 16 2017 at 00:51):

One style that explanation could go in that I think works well in some settings forms: just describe the consequences as fact (and then when the user changes the setting, the description changes.) Like

Organization admins won't have access to take over other users' accounts or see their private messages. Good for open source projects, social groups, and volunteer organizations.

Steve Howell (Jun 16 2017 at 00:52):
