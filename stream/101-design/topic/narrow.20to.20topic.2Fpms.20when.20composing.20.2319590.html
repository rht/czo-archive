Tim Abbott (Apr 26 2022 at 20:04):

OK, here's a hopefully final version of this detail, which I've pushed to #19590 and will be deploying here shortly:

  • We display a second line in the tooltip when it is potentially confusing. This means (1) the message you're composing would appear in the current view and (2) the current view is a stream narrow, all-private-messages narrow, or all-messages narrow. The last two could be potentially confusing if all the messages on screen are in the conversation in question (E.g. all of your last 20 messages are PMs with a single individual).
  • We do not display a second line in other situations.
  • We avoid displaying the stream name, since that's hard to style, so the messages are, e.g., "Currently viewing the entire stream." or "Currently viewing all private messages."

This avoids all the complexity (and bugs!) with trying to provide descriptions for situations where this button's appearance won't be confusing in the first place, like if you're composing a private message while viewing a search view for messages in public streams containing the word "turtle". Which seems appropriate given that this line is just a refinement to settle folks' feeling of it being confusing that this button appears when they thought they were already looking at the view they're composing to.
