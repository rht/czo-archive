Hey folks! @Palash Singh Raghuwanshi has pointed out that some of the Welcome Bot messages don’t make sense in the mobile app (thanks for flagging!). I’ve done a pass of going through the bot's responses, to see how they could be modified to work OK for mobile app users.

I think the following messages are already OK: apps, profile, message formatting, help. The settings link in profile takes you to mobile web (where you have to separately log in), but that’s probably the best we can do for now.

Here are proposals for discussion for the other messages: I’ve added pointers to what to do on mobile but otherwise did not modify these from the current version.

  • theme: Go to Display settings to switch between the light and dark themes, pick your favorite emoji theme, change your language, and make other tweaks to your Zulip experience. You can customize your mobile app experience by clicking on your profile picture.

  • streams: In Zulip, streams determine who gets a message. They are similar to channels in other chat apps.
    Browse and subscribe to streams, or click # and navigate to All streams in the mobile app.

  • topics: In Zulip, topics tell you what a message is about. They are light-weight subjects, very similar to the subject line of an email.
    Check out Recent topics to see what's happening, or click on the drawer icon in the mobile app! You can return to this conversation by clicking "Private messages" in the upper left, or the people icon on mobile.
