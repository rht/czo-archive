Tim Abbott (May 03 2017 at 17:42):

I have a few thoughts on these ideas:

  • I think everyone agrees the "Group PMs" widget should go; it exists only because group PMs didn't used to be in "Private messages" at all.
  • Active vs. inactive is quite useful in organizations with a lot of streams, because it means that you don't suffer any material inconvenience from being subscribed to an extra stream that is dormant right now (but might start having traffic again later).
  • I agree that it'd be really good to have "private messages" default-expanded at least to a few threads, and to not have the duplication of "private messages" with the user list in the right sidebar. But I have some concerns about moving private messages and mentions to the right sidebar that I think are worth talking about:
    • If the page is too narrow to fit both sidebars, right now the experience is pretty reasonable, because the left sidebar has everything important and needed to account for all the unread messages. We'd need to think carefully about how to make the experience still feel good if we moved those to the right sidebar. For that reason, I'd also not be excited about moving mentions and starred messages over. I think a potentially valid approach would be in that responsive mode to try to fit both the sidebars' content in the left side, either with a tab switcher or with them stacked above each other.
    • It's nice that one can catch up on all unread messages entirely looking at the left sidebar area, rather than having to look back and forth across the screen.
  • Rishi and I chatted a bit yesterday about Steve's idea of sorting unread streams in the left sidebar to the top, below pinned streams. A concern that came up is that doing so could make it hard to find a stream you're looking for when scanning the list (if your immediate goal is to open that stream and send a message to it, not to catch up on unread messages). Also, it would mean that the streams list re-sorts quite frequently when new messages come in, which might not look great and could be annoying when you're trying to click on a specific stream to have it potentially move on you.
