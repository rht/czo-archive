Tim Abbott (Mar 15 2021 at 01:00):

I think we definitely need to do something to better manage cases where we have a large number of authentication backends enabled (4+).

I'm wary of the idea of doing the horizontal buttons, though, for a few reasons:

  • "Log in with Google" buttons types have a standard shape/text across many websites, and we currently follow that convention.
  • Many organizations, especially self-hosted, might have only 1-2 authentication backends enabled, and the current design handles that case really well.
  • Changing to squares would create a different design issue, with handling more than can fix in the horizontal space (I guess we'd line-wrap?)

Tim Abbott (Mar 15 2021 at 01:01):

What size window is the screenshot starting this thread from? It doesn't look like a standard/common monitor shape, and I'm not sure we should be optimizing the design of our login page around tiny screens/windows.
