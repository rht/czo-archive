I'm not in love with our current logo, but for Brock: what I mean is that the current slot has space for say a 30px by 200px logo. Let's say we add an option that allows orgs to upload a replacement logo, and we advertize it as roughly a 30px by 200px spot.

Then, say a few months later we change the design of the nav bar area so that now there is only room for a 30px by 100px logo. All of our users will now have to go change their uploaded logos, which is a fairly big annoyance (i.e. more likely is that we will just never change the size of that whitespace.)

Or, let's say a few months later we want to change that corner to be a workplace switcher, or settings dropdown, or anything else. I think allowing custom logos there will make it harder to make such a change.

An alternative would be to just use the realm avatar there and put the name of the org in text.
