Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (May 19 2023 at 16:45):

@Felix My interpretation of those filters is that they're elements to help build up more complex search expressions, eg. is:starred is:dm banana. I believe that was Alya's point regarding 'if you want to combine it with keywords or other filters', which I attempted to clarify earlier too.

My take on the top left corner is to be shortcuts to those most frequently-used filters - excepting recent conversations and drafts, which are different types of view.

I've advocated in the past for a "streams" (ie. 'all my streams' in your words) shortcut/menu item/hotkey to parallel the private/direct message case. That already exists to a degree with the STREAMS title (eg. showing unreads), but previous discussion has erred away from cluttering the UI with eg. unreads for all stream messages there.

In general, there's only so much space that's practical to use here, vertically, since for now this section is a fixed height and doesn't scroll. However, there is design work afoot to change this :)
