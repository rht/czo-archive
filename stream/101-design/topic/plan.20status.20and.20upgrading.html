Topic: plan status and upgrading

Alya Abbott (Jun 03 2022 at 20:08):

Hey! As came up recently in a support request, Zulip does not make it convenient to figure out what Zulip Cloud plan your organization is on, and how to change it.

At present, owners of Zulip Cloud Free orgs see a link to plans in the gear menu:
menu-free.png

Owners of Zulip Cloud Standard orgs see a billing link instead:

menu-standard.png

However, until you go to /plans or the billing page, it's really not obvious what plan you're currently on. Contrast this with Slack, which makes it clear upfront in the org drop-down menu:

Screen-Shot-2022-06-02-at-3.41.18-PM.png

I think we should also make it more clear in-app (a) what plan you're on, and (b) how to upgrade (or downgrade). What are some ideas on how to approach it? A couple of directions that come to mind:

  1. Adding a "Zulip Cloud plan" (or something similar) section to org settings, probably as part of the "Organization settings" panel.
  2. Having the info be more clear/prominent/explicit in the gear menu dropdown.

What do folks think?
