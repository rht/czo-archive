My suggestion would be to develop the bot for your own use, but pull out fhir.org-specific details into a config file in cases where it's easy / obvious how to do that. Greg Price (Jun 09 2018 at 01:31):

I think the hard parts for a bot like this will be product/UI questions, not technical; so you'll want to optimize for easily making tweaks to it as you iterate and have ideas about how it could work better for you.

Greg Price (Jun 09 2018 at 01:35):

If by doing that you succeed at working out a solution that people in the fhir.org chat find really works well for you and keep coming back to, I think a lot of communities may be interested in getting something like that for themselves!
