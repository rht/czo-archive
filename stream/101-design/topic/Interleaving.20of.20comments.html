Neil Pilgrim (neiljp) (Dec 20 2020 at 00:33):

This is a stream view - you can zoom into (view) one of the topics by clicking on its title. There are shortcut keys for this - s/S will zoom in/out (in zulip-terminal we also added z for this to toggle in/out). In the webapp (and presumably desktop?) you can do alt-left/right to go back/forward between views too, just like going through web page history.
