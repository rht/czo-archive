Alya Abbott (Jun 29 2021 at 22:27):

My perspective is that work that impacts what Alex Dehnert describes should be our highest priority within the category of work on polish. From talking to customers, it definitely feels like there's a first impressions effect that is impacting Zulip's growth. The later-stage impact for organizations that do adopt Zulip is less obvious. That's why I'm especially excited about projects that affect the impression we give to new users, including making it easier to join streams and compose messages, changing the font size/spacing, and figuring how how to staff modernizing the overall look & feel.

We're always making choices about where to direct limited resources. There are different levels to have the discussion at, from priorities for iterating on specific features, to high-level perspective on how to distribute our efforts. If folks are concerned that we're under-investing in certain areas compared to others, in large part that's feedback that should be directed to Tim and myself, so that we can discuss and take those ideas into account.
