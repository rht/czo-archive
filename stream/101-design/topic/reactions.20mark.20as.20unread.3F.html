Topic: reactions mark as unread?

Alya Abbott (May 06 2022 at 15:46):

Hey! There's a user problem where sometimes you're waiting for a response on some message (e.g. an approval of some proposal), and there's no good way to notice that you got a response if it comes in the form of an emoji reaction (e.g. a thumbs up).

A possible solution is to mark your own message as unread when it receives a reaction. We could have this be a user setting.

I could also imagine a "watch this message" option (on your messages or anyone else's) that would mark that specific message as unread when someone reacts.

Thoughts?

Tim Abbott (May 06 2022 at 22:13):

I like the idea of having a setting with 3 options:

  • Never mark messages as unread due to a reaction.
  • Mark messages you sent as unread when the first other user reacts to it.
  • Mark messages you sent as unread whenever another user reacts to it.

And I'd imagine the middle one would be the default.
