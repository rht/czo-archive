Rishi Gupta (Oct 04 2018 at 20:59):

Should stream email notifications be "notifications always, even when on-line"? I feel like the only cases I know for email notifications are:

  • Easy plug-in to compliance (e.g. if you've already got your email system hooked in to your compliance stuff)
  • Getting stragglers on board ("you don't have to download yet another app. You can use Zulip from your email")

As a V2 we can add features like "wait 5 minutes before sending me an email notification", but I think that is actually a secondary use case.

Tim Abbott (Oct 05 2018 at 16:59):

We could certainly change that and then see whether people complain about "stream email notifications" being spammy (I would expect people to find them that way once they reconnect). But I think before making a change of this form, we should debug the thing you mentioned in #issues in case there's a bug here.
