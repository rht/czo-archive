I think the terms we currently use are:

Profile 1: Profile summary (help center), abbreviated to "Summary" as a column header in custom profile fields settings.

Profile 2: Full profile (link from profile 1), or sometimes just "profile" (see https://zulip.com/help/view-someones-profile).
