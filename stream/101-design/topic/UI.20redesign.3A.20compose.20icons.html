Thanks for working on the compose icons! Here's my personal reactions to them:

  • The Preview and Link icons aren't immediately recognizable to me as what they're supposed to be -- not sure I can readily express why.
  • The eyes on the emoji icon feel hard to make out (they kinda disappear for me below a certain size); maybe they'd benefit from a few more pixels. I think the ? icon in the top-right shares this issue.
  • The video call icon looks too square to me; maybe it could be less tall preserving the rest of the design?
  • Is the person with a checkmark meant to be a poll? It's not recognizable to me. I guess the problem is that the natural icons for poll and TODO are very similar. https://help.twitter.com/en/using-twitter/twitter-polls suggests Twitter uses a sideways bar graph for their polls icon; that might be a good direction since that's somewhat standard and we don't have a graphing feature it would compete with.
  • The scroll icon, is also a bit mysterious, but I think that's probably fundamental to the fact that this isn't a standard icon for the concept. I think it looks quite nice, and presumably you can get the label "Spoiler" via hover.
  • The numbered/bulleted list icons feel hard to parse for me; the unordered list reminds me a bit of a dot next to a hamburger menu. I think I'd be happier with a model similar to GitHub's where they have dots next to each line for \ul, and 2 numbers for \ol.
  • The rest look good to me.
