Tim Abbott (Dec 02 2022 at 19:12):

I think this was actually an intentional decision. I don't know what's best but here's a few interaction details that motivated this:

  • In the right sidebar, when you hover over a user row, we display a tooltip with extra details. Those tooltips can feel awkward when you're hovering the space between rows.
  • It can be more annoying for a misclick on the boundary between rows to take you to the wrong view than to do nothing.
