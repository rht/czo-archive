Tim Abbott (Apr 14 2019 at 19:26):

I think it's worth thinking about the different environments one might see:

  • In a corporate community where most users are active, the number of total users is actually pretty useful for understanding what's going on.
  • In an open source community like this one where you have tons of inactive users, I think it's still really useful to see the difference between 4, 27, 350, and 11K (all of which are approximate sizes of streams in this organization).
  • Trying to do something with active users is difficult to specify clearly -- do you want users from the last day? Last week? Etc. And what is active -- logged in? Read a message? Sent a message? We can think about that down the line, but I think we should consider trying to do something around active users to be out of scope for now.
