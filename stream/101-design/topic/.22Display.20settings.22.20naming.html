Topic: "Display settings" naming

Tim Abbott (Apr 07 2021 at 18:27):

Do we want to rename "Display settings" in the gear menu to "User interface"? I think structurally, it happens that essentially all of our user interface settings control how something is displayed, but that seems like a more clear label for this. Also, it's the only menu option that has "settings" in it, which feels redundant; they are all settings.

image.png

(Maybe worth a bit of research on what other products call this, in case there's a better phrasing)

(We'd probably change the API as well in follow-up work once we've changed the user-facing labels).
