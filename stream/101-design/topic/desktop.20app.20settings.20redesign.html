Cedric Amaya (Oct 22 2017 at 19:23):

Hey everyone, as part of my contribution for the Outreachy program I wanted to redesign the Settings page for the Electron desktop apps. Wanting to make the page as user-friendly as possible as well as giving it a clean design, I decided to use the Bulma CSS framework (https://bulma.io).

I've included some screenshots of the proposed design - keep in mind this is just a proof-of-concept and not 100% complete. If you'd like to play around with the page, feel free to do so: http://cedricamaya.me/zulip-electron-redesign/pages/general.html - most of the button are interactive. Would love to hear what you think!
