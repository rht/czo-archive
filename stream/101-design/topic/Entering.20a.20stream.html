Rishi Gupta (Oct 03 2018 at 23:47):

My impression is that the "straightforward" solution of starting at the first unread is technically non-trivial. It could be worth working on if it was the right long-term direction, but I think what the mobile app does is the right long term direction (nice job to mobile for figuring it out :)).

Specifically, there are two modes:

  • Catching up, where you are presented with a list of unread topics (organized by stream), not a list of streams. You click topic by topic (or use n or whatever), and are never forced to click on anything that lands you in an interleaved view.
  • Everything else, for which you would use the webapp's current left sidebar
