Topic: "manage" vs. "administer" groups

Alya Abbott (Jun 08 2023 at 21:52):

Tim Abbott asked me to kick off a thread about the choice of "manage group" vs. "administer group" as the terminology for being able to change group data (name/description, permissions, membership).

In #25929, I used the term "manage group" because:

  • I wanted to avoid confusion between this and the administrator role.
  • It felt more natural to me.
  • It's also a term we use to label the "Manage streams" menu (which of course could be changed).

Starting this thread in case folks have other thoughts on this, though. It affects how we'll want to name variables in the codebase as well.
