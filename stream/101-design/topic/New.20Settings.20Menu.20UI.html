Gittenburg (Jun 27 2020 at 07:42):

Feedback to the screenshot:

  • Aligning labels left and inputs all the way to the right is horrible from an UX standpoint since your eyes need to travel a long way (see the blue lines in my screenshot) and it's easy to loose the current line, even with your borders.

  • I don't find the rounded border to be an improvement, especially since it introduces an ugly gap (highlighted in red in my screenshot). I think our current gapless squared active section highlighting looks better.

  • I am uncertain about the toggle buttons ... they feel more heavy compared to checkboxes. On desktop I think checkboxes are generally more common. It might make sense to switch to toggle buttons on mobile because of the bigger click area.
