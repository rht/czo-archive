Rishi Gupta (Oct 28 2017 at 17:09):

What would folks think of the ability to create a fast demo realm?
I'm imagining a button on /hello, or somewhere, that says "Try it"
Clicking it creates an account with a made up username, subdomain, etc (like subdomain random-word-234534.server.tld, email noreply-134123@server.tld, fake name, avatar, etc).
You then get dumped right into our onboarding hotspot tutorial, where you get to see how the product looks, what we mean by threading, etc.

Steve Howell (Oct 28 2017 at 17:17):

I like it! Here's a variation on your idea:

  • We set up a good newbie realm called newbie.zulip.org that has real streams and human babysitters.
  • That realm has a special button to create-your-own realm. You can only click this once, but when you do, it creates a demo realm with your actual email. (Forget about the minor technical challenges here, such as duplicate emails.)

Steve Howell (Oct 28 2017 at 17:26):

I basically want users to experience two situations:

  • what does a functioning realm look like?
  • how does the realm onboarding experience feel? (particularly useful for potential admins)

It's probably also nice to have a little bit of gating before you get your own demo realm, just so we're not spinning up thousands of them when get slash-dotted.
