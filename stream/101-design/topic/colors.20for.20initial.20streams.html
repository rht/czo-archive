Armaan Ahluwalia (Aug 16 2018 at 20:15):

I feel like a project like this is sorely needed. I just wanted to put down some thoughts here on choosing colours and this is going to sound very wishy washy so feel free to ignore this message if you think design is about “making stuff pretty” :slight_smile:

Every colour is associated with certain emotions. I think our goal while picking colors should take into account what emotion the stream evokes and what colour uniquely identifies that emotion. To give an example the announce stream is usually surrounded by an air of excitement and celebration. We could pick a much more vibrant, exuberant colour for that that is more “striking”.

With this in mind I think we should pick colours on 2 axes -

1) The colour should generally bring about the feeling that stream evokes in a majority of cultures in our demographic
2) How striking/vibrant that colour is should be chosen by taking into account what order it meets the eye in comparison with other colours in the palette. I believe this has to do the with the wavelength of light in the spectrum ( perhaps my physics is off here ) but basically that means red meets the eye first as compared to say blue.

Armaan Ahluwalia (Aug 16 2018 at 20:16):

So for streams which we want people to see first we should choose colours higher up in that chart like reds and oranges and pinks and for less urgent or lower priority streams lower down likes blues greens pastels etc

Based on what @Boris Yankov said in https://chat.zulip.org/#narrow/stream/101-design/topic/Unread.20Count.20Styling
He feels like the colors are only marginally useful since when subscribed to a large number of streams, they tend to become very similar to each other.

With this in mind I wonder if it would be a useful experiment to allow streams to be associated with both a color and an icon from a predefined set ( or pre-vetted source ) of icons. I'm thinking something akin to how a lot of design systems use a colour and the initials for a unique identification symbol. ( Like the letter MT within a purple circle ). But perhaps we could associate it with an icon and a color which could serve to make them more unique?
