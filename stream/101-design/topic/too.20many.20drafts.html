Vaibhav Rabber (Jan 25 2019 at 19:58):

This is regarding issue #11217 that @Steve Howell created.

There can be two reasons for cluttering of drafts box:
1. Writing something and getting distracted, so clicking outside compose box (which in turn saves the message as a draft)
2. People who just store too many drafts (but they do care about them)

It is indeed required to clear up the draft box but setting a limit based on number seems a bit irrational here.
The option where we can keep the most recent drafts (say 4 or 5) in the box and show others when the user clicks “Show more” seems reasonable.

It also seems necessary to clear up the clutter. One solution that I thought of was to notify the user after a certain time (less than 30 days) (but that just seems too much for just drafts).
Maybe a subtle way to do this is by sending a private message to the user to check for unnecessary drafts.

Any thoughts?
