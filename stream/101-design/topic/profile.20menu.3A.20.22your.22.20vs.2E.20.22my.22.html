Topic: profile menu: "your" vs. "my"

Alya Abbott (Aug 22 2022 at 19:11):

Hey! When you view your own profile menu, we're currently inconsistent between different options about the pronouns we use to refer to the user. In particular, we mostly use "you" / "yourself", but one option uses "myself":

Screen-Shot-2022-08-22-at-11.52.24-AM.png

What do folks think?
