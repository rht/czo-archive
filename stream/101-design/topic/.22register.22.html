Topic: "register"

Greg Price (Dec 12 2017 at 04:12):

When talking about a user creating an account, most of our UI says "register", "registration", etc.

That term feels a bit abstract and formal to me, and I don't think I see it used in most places on the Web -- most sites prefer "sign up", which to me feels simpler and more direct. E.g., look (in an incognito window) at facebook.com, twitter.com, community.keyboard.io (which is an install of Discourse), or discordapp.com. All say "Sign Up".

Greg Price (Dec 12 2017 at 04:13):

So I think we should probably make a pass through our user-facing text and edit all mentions of "register" and friends to "sign up" etc.

Thoughts?

Greg Price (Dec 12 2017 at 04:14):

It's a bit hard to grep directly for this, because "register" is also the term we use in most places in the code and URLs, and that'll be more work to change. But grepping through static/locale/de/ (or any other locale) works well.
