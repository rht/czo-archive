Armaan Ahluwalia (Sep 12 2018 at 15:36):

Although I do hear what all of you are saying, I think I have to preface these changes with a few points -

1) From a design perspective, I felt - both when I first started using zulip, as well as when I review the design - the unread counts have too much impact in the visual hierarchy. They need to be toned down and I feel the new design achieves this.

2) Unread counts here in zulip are less akin to the images @Steve Howell presented and more like something you might find in your email inbox ( See attached image ). This is because we are not funneling all notifications into one "count" rather these are more an aggregation of unread messages in different parts of the application. Therefore I don't feel they need to stick to unread count styling seen on app badges as referenced by some of you.

3) I feel perhaps there might be an element of resistance to change at work here. I firmly believe in the design philosophy of experimentation and specially when it comes to design too much theorizing and not enough experimentation leads to stagnation. Couldn't we just give it a shot on the realm and take a poll at the end of some time period?
