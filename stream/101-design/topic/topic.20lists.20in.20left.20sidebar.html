I just merged a change (89ff62dafa9dbc47d935577d7b176e3d1634b7f6) that tweaks the left sidebar's behavior when looking at a stream where you have a lot of unread topics:

  • We now only show up to 8 topics without clicking "more topics".
  • "more topics" shows an unread count to communicate how many unread messages there are.

This should provide a much nicer experience if you click into a muted stream (or if you're not keeping up with a Zulip community and so have tons of unreads by default).
