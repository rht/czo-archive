Topic: edit topic of multiple messages

Alex Claydon (Jun 07 2021 at 06:08):

Our team has quickly grown to love (and depend on!) Zulip. However, one request that comes up often is the ability, on the desktop/web app, to select multiple messages in a topic and bulk re-allocate them to another topic (and/or another stream).

If we understand the Zulip paradigm correctly, then re-allocation of messages between topics is something that should be happening on a not-infrequent basis. For example, you're having a conversation on one topic but then realise that something that has been said mid-conversation deserves its own topic. However, at the moment you either have to re-allocate message-by-message - which quickly gets fiddly and repetitious - or you have to re-allocate all messages in the topic (falling either (i) before and after the selected message, or (ii) after the selected message).

The ability to hold down, e.g., the Command key and click-select multiple messages and then re-allocate all of those selected messages with one edit would be very powerful. Perhaps, in the fullness of time, the ability to simply drag-drop those selected messages onto a new topic could even be introduced.

I've had a search within this #design stream, as well as a look at the official documentation and couldn't find anything pertinent but apologies if this functionality exists but I've missed it.
