Ben Reeves (Apr 09 2018 at 17:50):

Hi. As I was using zulip today, I had a question to ask but didn't know which stream to ask it in. So I wanted to look through the descriptions of all the streams in my left sidebar and see which one would be appropriate. However, I found myself dissatisfied with the amount of effort needed to discover the purpose of a stream.

Currently, I have to open up the stream settings in order to see what a stream is about. Not only is this one click too many for me, but it is not very intuitive in my mind.

I searched the chat and found #8707 (put the stream description in the stream popover), which I think would be a great step in the right direction; however, as Tim pointed out, this would be problematic for longer descriptions.

What I would like to see is something along the lines of what Slack does with the "channel topic". Whenever you are viewing a channel, the channel topic is displayed at the top.

Screen-Shot-2018-04-09-at-1.48.01-PM.png

I'd like to hear people's thoughts on this.

Ben Reeves (Apr 09 2018 at 17:52):
