Topic: open compose box on narrow to private messages.

Dinesh Ch (Jun 20 2020 at 17:10):

From all messages view, When we click on a header like You and someone that narrows to private messages with that person, and opens compose box, but it's not the same for clicking on a topic name from all messages view. Is this intentional for some reason like "people usually narrow to private messages to read?"

Tim Abbott (Jun 20 2020 at 21:53):

Yeah, this is a design choice based on the fact that most messages in a stream/topic are ready many times per message sent, which is a structural fact of large group conversation, while looking at a PMs narrow is very likely to result in wanting to send a message.
