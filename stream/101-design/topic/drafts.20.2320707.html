We want to be considerate of people who either can't use a mouse or choose not to use a mouse. If you are a keyboard-centric user, then it's helpful if the formatting help is easy to reach without tabbing past eight other icons.

Steve Howell (Feb 02 2022 at 15:26):

Steve Howell (Feb 02 2022 at 15:35):
