Stream: design

Topic: all messages

Rishi Gupta (Jul 25 2017 at 01:14):

This was brought up in #issues, but reposting here.
What would folks think of renaming "Home" to "All messages"? That would be less confusing to beginners, and set expectations a bit better.

One downside is that it's not quite all messages, in that it doesn't include muted streams and topics. So we would have to make sure that isn't confusing.

Joshua Pan (Jul 25 2017 at 01:16):

It's not all messages, but "Home" doesn't really encapsulate that it doesn't include muted streams or topics either.

James Rowan (Jul 25 2017 at 01:17):

I think "All messages" is better (and probably more accurate than something like "overview"). I don't think the fact that streams/topics that got muted don't show up is likely to be confusing--when you mute a stream or topic you're asking not to see those messages any more and don't really expect to see them in "all the messages you've received" anyway.

Steve Howell (Jul 25 2017 at 01:21):

There's a search term called is:all that truly brings in "all" messages (which is in quotes because it's only another sort-of-false definition of "all" that doesn't include messages you can't see but that were sent in the realm), but we've already started to deprecate that, I believe, in search suggestions. So I agree with everybody here. I agree with Rishi on the basic proposal, I agree with Joshua that "Home" is already an imperfect name, and I agree with James that the likelihood of confusion is small. I also like "overview" as a possible alternative name, since I think that gets to the heart of why some people like the view.

James Rowan (Jul 25 2017 at 01:24):

My one worry with "overview" is that people would expect it to do some sort of summarizing/only showing you the "important stuff" when really it's everything that got sent. It feels a bit information dense for an "overview" in the usual sense of the word--the thing I like about it/feel is missing from Slack is that it's a "global view" moreso than an "overview"

Steve Howell (Jul 25 2017 at 01:28):

I call it the "interleaved view." But I don't propose that as the user-facing name, necessarily. You're right--it's not an overview. (We actually could use a nice "overview" type of view that is a little more than the left sidebar provides but not as busy as the home view). The benefit of the interleaved view, as I perceive it, is that it's totally real time. You don't have anything happen asynchronously, but it's still somewhat organized.
