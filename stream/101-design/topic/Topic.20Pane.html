Topic: Topic Pane

Armaan Ahluwalia (Sep 24 2018 at 12:23):

I think it would be really useful to add a topic pane to zulip. Here's what I mean -

1) Move buddy list to a widget on the bottom right much like you see on facebook.
2) Add a 'Topic Pane' to the right sidebar in place of the buddy list. This means that whatever view you're in you're able to easily browse through the topics in that context organized by the time of last message.

So for -
All messages view you would see a list of all topics and allow you to easily browse through all topics in zulip across all streams. Particularly useful when reading back through the history of a realm or catching up since you can easily mark topics you dont care about as read with right-click.

Private, messages starred messages and mentions - This could have a list of messages or topics but is more of a bonus to have this. Not particularly necessary but still nice to have

Streams - A list of topics within the stream chronologically much like you see in the left sidebar currently.

Search Results - This is something I've wanted since the beginning of using zulip. A list of topics for the search results page.

I believe this could be a significant improvement to the user-experience of zulip and would like to get your thoughts. @Rishi Gupta @Tim Abbott @Steve Howell

Armaan Ahluwalia (Sep 24 2018 at 16:11):

It’s in line with the fact that zulip is indeed topic-oriented as opposed to message-oriented and makes most use-cases easier.

Armaan Ahluwalia (Sep 24 2018 at 17:30):

Thanks for your input @Boris Yankov . I believe the following user flows would be far improved by this feature -

1. Catching up on a lot of missed messages

Usually a user scrolls through all messages or within a narrow. In either case, the new feature would allow you to more easily “scroll” or rather read through a bunch of missed messages By either clicking on that topic to read it or clicking on an icon to mark as read aka skipping that topic completely. This is an improvement over having to manually scroll through a topic they don’t have interest in which I often find myself doing when catching up. A mark as read icon could appear when hovering the topic on the right sidebar, or could be an additional menu on right click.

2. Discovery / Search Since topics are first class citizens in zulip, it follows that their discovery should also be just as easy. Having the right sidebar on the search page makes topics listable discoverable much easier.

The overarching idea with this feature is essentially enabling topics to become truly first class citizens within the design.
Streams are omnipresent within the app no matter what action you’re taking. The same should go for topics since the idea behind them is to abstract a bunch of information together. It follows that this level of granularity deserves a more permanent and reliable form of interaction in the UI.
